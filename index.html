Five Lions Mentoring was founded on a simple principle: students learn best from those who have recently walked in their shoes.

We are a collective of high-achieving students dedicated to uplifting the next generation. Every mentor on our roster is an exceptional student, maintaining A averages in rigorous coursework.

But we aren't just academics. Our team is dynamic and well-rounded. We are athletes, tech-enthusiasts, and leaders in our communities. This allows us to connect with children on multiple levels, fostering a mentorship dynamic that goes far beyond traditional tutoring.

Our Services

Tailored mentoring programs designed to help your child excel academically, prepare for the future, and develop physical and technical skills outside the classroom.

Academic Excellence

Subject-specific tutoring for students in 1st through 6th grade. We go beyond the basics—guiding students through math up to 3 levels above their grade, honing sophisticated essay writing skills, and introducing advanced concepts in finance to build early literacy.

School Entrance Prep

Targeted preparation for rigorous magnet programs and prestigious private academies like St. Mark's. We provide strategic studying techniques, interview prep, and teach practical tech skills—like crafting highly effective slide presentations—so your child stands out.

Beyond the Classroom

Education doesn't stop at a desk. Our dynamic mentors offer specialized training to develop well-rounded skills, from helping students make and excel on competitive athletic teams to mastering technical abilities.

  • Soccer Practices
  • Agility Training
  • Safe Weightlifting

  • Competitive Robotics
